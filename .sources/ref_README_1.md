## Key Findings Summary

### Market Opportunity
- North American renewable energy construction market: $77.8B annually, growing to $133.8B by 2027
- 58.7% of mid-size contractors planning renewable expansion (next 2 years)
- Green-qualified projects represent 36% of construction biddable market

### Competitive Advantage
- Contractors with green credentials: 2.04× higher win rate on qualified projects
- Premium capture range: 2.4%–5.4% depending on credential level and specialization
- Repeat business: 61% for green clients vs. 29% baseline (2.1× improvement)

### Financial Returns
- Green credential investment: $330,000 Year 1
- Year 1 payback: 2.1 months
- Year 1 ROI: 120.4%
- 3-year cumulative incremental revenue: $6.37M

### Strategic Timeline
- First-mover advantage window: 12–18 months
- Early premium capture: 4.8% (declining to 2.2% as market matures)
- Market inflection point: Passing now (36% market penetration → 50%+ projected 2027)
